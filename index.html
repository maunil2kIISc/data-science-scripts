District - Taluka wise 10 Crore Plantation Status (Maharashtra)

A small exploratory analysis of the dataset (as on 08-09-2025)

About this repository

This repository contains an exploratory analysis of the "District - Taluka wise 10 Crore Plantation Status" dataset released by the Forest Department of Maharashtra (dataset snapshot: 08-09-2025).

Files in this repo:

  • Plantation_2.csv — raw dataset (district/taluka planting statistics)
  • Research_methods_assignment_5.ipynb — Jupyter notebook with analysis and visualizations

Original data source: data.gov.in

What the analysis does

  • Loads Plantation_2.csv, coerces numeric fields and strips formatting (commas, extra whitespace).
  • Creates visualizations: scatter plot (planned seedlings vs pits dug), box plots of distributions, and a top-20 bar chart of seedlings planted.

Key observations

  • No. of pits dug shows a linear trend for the majority of districts, though some districts recorded seedling planting without pits being dug.
  • From box plots: median counts for planned seedlings, pits dug, and seedlings planted are roughly 100,000, 75,000, and 60,000 respectively.
